Ahmed Fathy Abdelkhalek

I am a proactive and diligent B.Sc. Mechatronics Engineering graduate from the German University in Cairo with experience in robotics, control, and R&D. I possess strong mathematical, analytical, and creative thinking skills with fluency in Arabic and English and can work independently. I am keen on pursuing a career in mechatronics engineering with a focus on designing, developing, and enhancing robotic and mechatronic systems.

I did my bachelor thesis at the German University in Cairo under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Ayman A. El-Badawy. The thesis models the kinematics and dynamics of a novel 3-DoF Delta parallel robot and applies both linear and nonlinear control laws for trajectory tracking. This is in order to control the movement of nano-carriers in drug delivery applications. The study was published at the IEEE Novel Intelligent and Leading Emerging Sciences Conference (NILES) and received the 3rd Best Paper Award. You can read it here.
