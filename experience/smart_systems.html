Project Case Study: Bathtub Painting

This was a simulation for one of our clients that wanted to implement a KUKA robot with their factory line to paint bathtubs ranging from 70x70 cm to 150x150 cm. To implement this solution, I started by selecting a suitable KUKA robot that can cover the wide range of bathtub sizes. This ended up being the KR 90 R3100 extra robot combined with a KP1-V single-axis positioner. This allowed the robot to cover all sizes without any singularity to ensure smooth and consistent speed when painting, which is crucial for the process.

Additionally, I created a layout to optimize the robot's productivity by having two static blocks that hold one bathtub each such that the robot is painting in one station while the two other stations are being loaded.
